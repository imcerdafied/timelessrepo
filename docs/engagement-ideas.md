# Engagement Ideas

These are experience ideas that fit the Phunware hospitality framing and can become reusable platform modules across Atlantis and future properties.

## High-Impact Guest Moments

- TimeLens selfie keepsake: let a guest place themselves into another era, then save or share the image as a branded card with a deep link back into the property experience.
- Then-and-now camera slider: hold up the phone and drag a temporal split line across the live camera view to compare the current place with a past or future layer.
- Character quests: give each historical or operational character a tiny mission, such as ask the governor for a pardon, help the dockmaster choose a storm plan, or find the marine biologist's reef clue.
- Passport stamps: reward visited zones, completed conversations, TimeLens captures, and shares with collectible property stamps.
- Story trails: package layers into short walks, such as Pirate Nassau, The Making of Paradise Island, Behind the Mirage, or Reef Rescue.
- Living postcards: generate a shareable artifact with the guest selfie, era label, property mark, QR code, and a suggested caption.

## Property and Commerce Tie-Ins

- Affinity-aware offers: after a guest shows interest in pirates, marine conservation, food, nightlife, or family activities, surface a relevant experience, booking, restaurant, tour, or retail item.
- Concierge handoff: turn a character interaction into a real action, such as reserve a cabana, book a snorkel tour, find the quietest beach, or plan a rainy-day route.
- Event-aware moments: tune On This Day content to actual resort programming, weather, occupancy, or nearby events.
- Group mode: families or conference groups can unlock shared trails and compare what each person discovered.

## Ambient Delight

- Proximity-triggered whispers: as a guest enters a zone, a short audio moment or character line appears without requiring a tap.
- Time weather: let the visual and audio treatment shift with actual time of day, tide, or weather.
- Mystery layers: hide a few discoverable moments behind repeat visits, long dwell, or physical QR markers.
- Staff stories: blend official history with behind-the-scenes operational stories from chefs, aquarists, dockmasters, and housekeepers.
